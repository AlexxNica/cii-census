internationalized substitute of Text::Wrap

This module is a substitution for Text::Wrap, supporting multibyte characters such as UTF-8, EUC-JP, and GB2312, fullwidth characters such as east Asian characters, combining characters such as diacritical marks and Thai, and languages which don't use whitespaces between words such as Chinese and Japanese.

It provides wrap().
