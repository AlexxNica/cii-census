Apache module for SSL and TLS encryption with GnuTLS

mod_gnutls provides SSL 3.0, TLS 1.0, and TLS 1.1 encryption with the GnuTLS library. It's similar in purpose to mod_ssl, but doesn't use OpenSSL.
